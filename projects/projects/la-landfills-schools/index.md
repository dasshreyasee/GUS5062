# Low-Income Areas with Schools Near Landfills  
### Los Angeles County GIS Analysis

This project maps schools located near methane-producing landfills in Los Angeles County.  
Using income data and school locations, the analysis highlights neighborhoods where environmental hazards and socioeconomic vulnerability overlap.

## Summary
This analysis reveals clusters of schools located near landfill sites, particularly in lower-income areas.  
Environmental justice concerns arise because students in these communities may face higher exposure to pollutants and other risks.
